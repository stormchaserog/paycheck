Paycheck

Crypto-native payroll on Solana — batch pay, tap-once yield-funded advances, and protocol revenue routed back to the workers who earned it.

One rail for every payment an onchain company makes. Pay contributors in seconds, route pump.fun/Meteora/Raydium rewards to the team, let contributors advance against their next paycheck. Every transaction verifiable on Solscan.

How it works

01
Payroll
Batch pay contributors in USDC. Sub-second. Every payment on-chain with a memo. Verifiable on Solscan.
02
Yield Advances
Contributors tap once to advance against their next paycheck. Idle balance yield auto-repays over 14 days. Zero worker fees.
03
Revenue Routing
Connect pump.fun, Meteora, Raydium fee wallets. Auto-route protocol revenue to payroll, advance pool, or treasury.
04
One Rail
Payroll + vendor pay + protocol rewards in a single control surface. Multi-team. Exportable. Built for onchain companies.

Built on Solana

Sub-second settlement. Near-zero fees. Composable with Phantom, Squads, and Reflect natively.
